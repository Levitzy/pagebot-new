# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 06-07-2025  

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- Messages you send to our bot through Facebook Messenger
- Any content, text, or commands you share with the bot

### 1.2 Information We Receive from Facebook
- Your Facebook user ID
- Your first and last name (if available in your Facebook profile)
- Your profile picture (if available and accessible)
- Message metadata (message IDs, timestamps)

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
- Bot interaction logs (for debugging and improving service)
- Message delivery status and response data
- Command usage statistics

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Respond to your messages and commands
- Provide bot functionality and services
- Improve and maintain our bot service
- Debug technical issues
- Ensure proper operation of bot commands

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- Message data is processed in real-time and may be temporarily stored for operational purposes
- User profile information is retrieved from Facebook as needed and not permanently stored
- Interaction logs may be retained for debugging and service improvement purposes
- We do not maintain long-term databases of your personal conversations

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of our service or users
- With Facebook/Meta as required for bot operation through their platform

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our bot operates on Facebook Messenger and uses Facebook's Graph API. Your interactions are subject to:
- Facebook's Data Policy
- Facebook's Terms of Service
- Facebook Messenger Platform policies

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:
- Secure API communications with Facebook
- Access token protection
- Limited data retention practices

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Stop using the bot at any time
- Block or unblock the bot on Facebook Messenger
- Request information about data we may have collected
- Report concerns about privacy or data handling

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy with an updated effective date.
